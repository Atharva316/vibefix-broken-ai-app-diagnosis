Login or Signup Broke in Your Vibe-Coded App — Diagnosis Steps

Auth breaks feel scary, but the safest first move is to locate the failed step: provider, callback, session, redirect, or policy.

Break type checklist

  • Signup works but login fails.
  • Login redirects to the wrong URL.
  • User appears logged out after refresh.
  • Dashboard loads but data is blocked.

What to check first

Check provider settings, callback URLs, session persistence, console errors, and whether production env vars differ from preview.

Paste-ready prompt

Do not rewrite the app. Login/signup broke in my AI-built app. Diagnose the auth flow step by step: provider, callback URL, session, redirect, and data access. Do not change database policies or env vars until you explain exactly why.
