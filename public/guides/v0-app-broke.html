v0 App Broke? Here's the First Thing to Check

v0 often generates strong UI fast, but breaks can happen when component boundaries, props, or imports shift.

Break type checklist

  • Component no longer renders.
  • Import path or export changed.
  • Layout broke after adding a new section.
  • One component change affected a shared wrapper.

What to check first

Check the exact component that changed, its imports, props, and parent layout. Avoid broad restyling until the component boundary is clear.

Paste-ready prompt

Do not rewrite the app. My v0-generated app broke after this UI/component change: [describe]. Identify the smallest component/import/prop/layout issue. Do not refactor unrelated files. Give me the smallest safe fix and a regression checklist.
