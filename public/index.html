Problem

AI-built apps break differently.

The code may work, but you may not fully understand why. Then one prompt, update, deploy, auth change, or database change breaks the app - and every new "fix this" prompt creates another problem.

Fix-break loops

Preview vs production

Auth/data confusion

Scope

Principles

What we'll never do

Never tell you to rewrite your whole app.

Most broken AI apps need scoped diagnosis, not a full rebuild.

Never touch your production database.

Database and auth changes need evidence, not panic prompts.

Never give advice we're not confident about.

We'll tell you when the diagnosis needs more information.

Never pretend certainty.

If confidence is low, the report says exactly what is missing.
